Understanding Legal Responsibilities

  • Data Protection Legislation
    • Data Security: Requirements for organisations to keep data secure and protect personal information.
  • Computer Misuse Legislation
    • Illegal Practices: Definitions of illegal practices such as hacking, unauthorized access, and malware distribution.
    • Applications: Applications and consequences of violating computer misuse laws.
  • Telecommunications Regulations
    • Lawful Business Practice: Interception of communications under lawful business practices.
    • Monitoring: Requirements that allow companies to monitor an employeeâ€™s communication and internet use while at work.
  • Fraud Legislation
    • Fraudulent Practices: Requirements to deal with services using IT-based methods to steal information for fraudulent purposes.
  • Health and Safety at Work Legislation
    • Duties of Employers: Responsibilities to ensure a safe working environment.
    • Duties of Employees: Responsibilities of employees to adhere to safety protocols.
    • Health and Safety Executive (HSE): Role and duties of the HSE in enforcing health and safety regulations.
    • General Prohibitions: General prohibitions and requirements under health and safety legislation.
