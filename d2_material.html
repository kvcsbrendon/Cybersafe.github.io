External Service Providers

Understanding ESP Agreements and Implications

  • External Service Provider Agreements
    • Cloud: Agreements for cloud services, including data storage and processing.
    • Hardware: Agreements for the provision and maintenance of hardware components.
    • Software: Agreements for software licensing, updates, and support.
  • Implications of ESP Agreements
    • Legal Ownership and Jurisdiction:
      • Geographical location of data storage and processing.
      • Data movement across borders.
      • Procedures when an agreement ends.
    • Security Protection:
      • Data security obligations.
      • Privacy and encryption requirements.
      • Liability for data breaches and data loss or damage (accidental or deliberate).
      • Disaster recovery procedures.
    • Dispute Resolution:
      • Statutory requirements for dispute resolution.
      • Challenges posed by data and processing residing in multiple jurisdictions.
    • Data Protection Laws: Many of these points are covered by data protection laws.
